Background on World History

This is a brief overview of world history.

Greek and Romans (800 BC-476 AD)

The writers of the classical era are Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle. The forum was a center of public debate and speaking in the Roman government.

Song Dynasty (960-1279 AD)

Gunpowder was one of the key inventions in the Song dynasty, originally used for fireworks. However, as time passed it was able to be used for weaponry in various conflicts.

Another key innovation was the use of Champa rice, a crop that could be harvested much more quickly as opposed to regular crops, allowing for a quick boost in the conomic structure through trade and markets.
